Sun shines brightly.
I squint my eyes,
I wink

We sleep
At a construction site
Abandoned.

Dust settles on your palm.
Cracks on moist asbestos
Remind me of old times

Stillness in the air
Sounds like static,
Kinda

At the end of time 
Will we still remember
This subtle quiet scent of peace
